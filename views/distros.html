Top 3 Neovim Distributions for Beginners

LazyVim

LazyVim uses the lazy.nvim package manager and comes with amazing defaults, making it a top choice for many Neovim beginners.

NvChad

NvChad offers a wide range of language servers preinstalled and provides a user-friendly experience for new users.

AstroNvim

AstroNvim is a community-driven Neovim distribution offering extensive plugin support, enhancing the user experience.
